### SCENE-071「未來的晶片」

**劇情位置**：第1卷第03章
**敘事說明**：林語青首次登場。她安靜走進程維倫的工作室，將一枚標記2091年時間戳的記憶晶片放在桌上，平靜地說：「你會想起來的。」程維倫困惑地盯著這枚來自未來的晶片。

**構圖與鏡頭**：
- 景別：中景轉特寫
- 焦距：50mm
- 景深：淺景深，聚焦桌面晶片，兩人前後虛化交替
- 構圖法：三分法，晶片置於下三分之一交叉點

**光線與色調**：
- 主光源：桌面全息投影的藍白光
- 色溫：冷調
- 主色：深藍、銀白、暗灰
- 氛圍：神秘、懸疑、安靜中帶壓迫

**人物互動**：
- 林語青：面無波瀾，嘴角微抿，指尖剛離開晶片，眼神穿透對方
- 程維倫：微皺眉，身體微微後傾，困惑中帶一絲不安
- 距離/張力：桌子寬度的距離，情感張力極高——陌生人說出不可能知道的事

**情緒張力**：★★★★☆ — 整部作品的情感引信，一句話打開潘朵拉之盒。

**選擇理由**：林語青的首次登場定義了全書的懸疑基調，未來晶片是貫穿全書的核心物件。

> **Prompt A（暖色生活動畫風）**：
> A young woman with black shoulder-length straight hair and an oversized white lab coat gently places a small glowing memory chip on a cluttered desk. Across the table, a young man in a black hoodie stares at the chip with confused eyes. Warm amber overhead light, soft painterly anime style, cozy yet mysterious workshop filled with tools and holographic screens, gentle dust motes in the air.

> **Prompt B（霓虹賽博朋克風）**：
> Neon-lit cyberpunk repair shop, a pale woman in a white lab coat slides a luminous blue memory chip across a metal workbench toward a messy-haired man in a black hoodie. The chip reads "2091" in holographic digits. Cold blue and electric teal reflections on wet surfaces, rain streaking the window behind them, dramatic rim lighting, tension thick in the air.

> **Prompt C（極簡向量插畫風）**：
> Flat vector illustration, split composition. Left side: silhouette of a woman in a white coat, arm extended placing a small diamond-shaped chip. Right side: silhouette of a man in a hoodie leaning back, question mark subtly formed in negative space above his head. Bold midnight blue background, glowing cyan chip as focal point, clean geometric editorial style.
