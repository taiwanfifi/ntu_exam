### SCENE-001「程維倫 正面肖像」

**劇情位置**：第一卷 / 概念圖
**敘事說明**：記憶修復師程維倫的標準肖像。地下街日光燈下，一個看起來什麼都不在乎、但手指上的疤痕說明一切的年輕人。

**構圖與鏡頭**：
- 景別：中景（胸口以上）
- 焦距：85mm 人像
- 景深：淺景深虛化背景
- 構圖法：中心對稱，臉部微偏三分線

**光線與色調**：
- 主光源：頭頂日光燈慘白光
- 色溫：冷調
- 主色：暗灰、慘白、黑色帽T
- 氛圍：疲憊、沉默

**人物細節**：
- 表情：眼皮微垂，瞳孔略失焦，嘴角既非笑也非愁——一種「省電模式」的臉
- 姿態：肩膀微駝，頭微微前傾
- 服裝細節：黑色帽T 帽沿磨白，領口鬆垮露出鎖骨，袖口有焊接灼痕
- 互動物件：右手食指舊燙疤清晰可見，指甲剪得極短

**情緒張力**：★★☆☆☆ — 平靜到近乎空洞，但那道疤暗示他比表面更深

**選擇理由**：主角第一印象至關重要。這張肖像要讓觀者感到「這個人很普通」，但目光會被那雙修復師的手和疤痕拉住，埋下好奇心。

> **Prompt A（暖色生活動畫風）**：
> Portrait of a 28-year-old Taiwanese man in a worn black hoodie, messy black hair sticking up, deep brown unfocused eyes, faint burn scar on right index finger. Soft overhead fluorescent light casting gentle shadows under his eyes. Muted grey background of an underground corridor. Warm painterly brushstrokes, soft edges like Violet Evergarden, melancholic serenity, delicate skin tones with subtle warmth beneath pallor.

> **Prompt B（霓虹賽博朋克風）**：
> Cyberpunk portrait of a gaunt young man in a black hoodie, pale skin lit by harsh fluorescent tubes above, faint neon bleed from unseen signs casting cyan edges on his jawline. Messy black hair, dead-tired brown eyes, visible burn scar on right index finger. Dark underground street behind him, exposed pipes, wet concrete. High contrast, Blade Runner mood, rain-mist atmosphere, electric blue rim light.

> **Prompt C（極簡向量插畫風）**：
> Flat vector portrait of a young man in black hoodie, simplified messy black hair as angular shapes, half-lidded eyes rendered as two dark ellipses. Muted grey-green background, single fluorescent light bar as white rectangle above. Burn scar on index finger highlighted in warm orange against cool palette. Bold outlines, limited four-color palette: charcoal, grey, pale skin, rust-orange accent. Clean editorial style.

### SCENE-002「程維倫 工作中側面」

**劇情位置**：第一卷 / 概念圖
**敘事說明**：程維倫坐在修復台前，三面螢幕的藍光把他的側臉照得像手術室裡的外科醫生。他正在修復一段破碎的記憶，指尖在虛擬介面上精準游移。

**構圖與鏡頭**：
- 景別：中景偏特寫（側面半身）
- 焦距：50mm 標準
- 景深：淺景深，螢幕上的文字微虛
- 構圖法：三分法，人物置右，左側螢幕光暈延伸

**光線與色調**：
- 主光源：三面螢幕藍光
- 色溫：冷調為主，皮膚帶微暖
- 主色：電子藍、深黑、肌膚蒼白
- 氛圍：專注、肅穆

**人物細節**：
- 表情：眼神極度銳利，瞳孔收縮，嘴唇微抿——像手術進行到關鍵步驟
- 姿態：上半身前傾，雙手懸在虛擬介面上方，手指微張如指揮家
- 服裝細節：帽T袖子推到手肘，露出細瘦但穩定的前臂
- 互動物件：虛擬介面上流動的記憶數據碎片，手指間穿梭的光粒子

**情緒張力**：★★★☆☆ — 這是他最接近「活著」的時刻，工作中的他比任何時候都鋒利

**選擇理由**：展現程維倫的核心技能和他工作時的變身感。平時無神的眼睛在藍光中變成手術刀，這個反差是角色魅力的核心。

> **Prompt A（暖色生活動畫風）**：
> Side profile of a young Taiwanese man at a triple-monitor repair station, blue screen light softly painting his focused face. Sleeves rolled up, slender fingers hovering over holographic interface with floating memory fragments like golden fireflies. Warm painterly rendering, gentle glow on skin, soft focus on background monitors showing data streams. Violet Evergarden-style luminous atmosphere, quiet intensity, amber highlights in blue light.

> **Prompt B（霓虹賽博朋克風）**：
> Cyberpunk side profile, pale young man bathed in intense blue light from three curved monitors. Eyes razor-sharp, pupils contracted, fingers dancing through holographic memory data — shattered light particles swirling around his hands. Dark underground workshop behind him, exposed wiring, green power indicator dots. Ghost in the Shell aesthetic, extreme blue-teal color grading, volumetric light rays from screens, digital rain reflections on skin.

> **Prompt C（極簡向量插畫風）**：
> Flat vector side profile of a man at a triple-screen workstation, face rendered in cool blue tones. Three monitor rectangles emit gradient blue glow. Geometric holographic interface elements float near simplified hands. Memory data as small diamond shapes in electric blue and white. Dark charcoal background, limited palette: midnight blue, cyan, pale cream, black. Clean lines, editorial composition, negative space on left.

### SCENE-003「程維倫 屋頂獨處」

**劇情位置**：第一卷 / 概念圖
**敘事說明**：程維倫坐在地下街Y區出口的鐵樓梯上，手裡拿著一杯珍珠奶茶半糖少冰。他仰望手扶梯上方——那裡是地面的世界，是陽光，是他不屬於的地方。

**構圖與鏡頭**：
- 景別：全景
- 焦距：35mm
- 景深：全景深
- 構圖法：對角線構圖，鐵樓梯從左下延伸到右上，人物坐在中段

**光線與色調**：
- 主光源：手扶梯上方透下的自然光（帶暖意）
- 色溫：混合（上方暖、下方冷）
- 主色：鏽橙鐵樓梯、上方金色光暈、下方灰綠
- 氛圍：孤獨、嚮往

**人物細節**：
- 表情：仰臉，眼睛微瞇像在看遠方的光，嘴角放鬆——罕見的不防備時刻
- 姿態：背靠鐵欄杆，一腳踩在下一階，手肘撐在膝蓋上，珍奶杯輕握
- 服裝細節：軍綠舊外套敞開，黑色帽T內搭，灰色工裝褲膝蓋處磨白
- 互動物件：半杯珍珠奶茶，珍珠沉在杯底，吸管斜插

**情緒張力**：★★★★☆ — 一個地下世界的人仰望地面，珍珠奶茶是他和「正常生活」最後的連結

**選擇理由**：這是程維倫最脆弱的畫面。鐵樓梯作為「邊界」的隱喻——他永遠坐在中間，不上不下。珍珠奶茶的溫暖對比鐵鏽的冰冷，視覺衝擊力極強。

> **Prompt A（暖色生活動畫風）**：
> A lonely young man sitting on rusted iron stairs in an underground passage, holding a half-drunk bubble tea, gazing upward at warm golden light streaming down from an escalator above. Military-green jacket draped open, black hoodie underneath. Rust-orange metal stairs contrast with soft amber light from the surface world. Painterly warmth, nostalgic atmosphere like a Makoto Shinkai quiet moment, dust particles floating in the light beam.

> **Prompt B（霓虹賽博朋克風）**：
> Cyberpunk wide shot of a figure sitting on industrial metal stairs in a dark underground passage, face tilted up toward distant surface light. Rusted iron railings, exposed pipes, condensation dripping. He holds a bubble tea cup, the only warm-toned object. Above him, escalator carries silhouettes into a golden neon glow. Stark contrast between dark teal underground and warm amber above. Blade Runner 2049 loneliness, wet metal reflections, volumetric god-rays.

> **Prompt C（極簡向量插畫風）**：
> Flat vector illustration of a small figure on diagonal iron stairs, looking up toward a bright geometric light source above. Bubble tea cup as a simple warm-orange circle in hand. Stairs rendered as bold rust-brown parallel lines cutting across dark grey-green background. Upper portion warm gold gradient, lower portion cool charcoal. Strong diagonal composition, minimal detail, four-color palette: rust, gold, charcoal, teal. Lonely editorial mood.

### SCENE-004「林語青 初登場」

**劇情位置**：第一卷第二章 / 關鍵場景
**敘事說明**：林語青第一次出現在程維倫工作室門口。白色大衣在昏暗走廊中格外突兀，背後的日光燈把她照成一個逆光剪影。她的目光穿過門縫，正在確認什麼。

**構圖與鏡頭**：
- 景別：全景（門框構圖）
- 焦距：35mm
- 景深：淺景深，前景門框微虛
- 構圖法：框中框，門框作為畫中畫邊界

**光線與色調**：
- 主光源：走廊日光燈（背光）
- 色溫：冷調
- 主色：白色大衣、走廊慘白、門框深黑
- 氛圍：神秘、不安

**人物細節**：
- 表情：目光穩定到不自然，像在「對焦」而非「看」，嘴唇閉合成一條直線
- 姿態：站姿筆直但不僵硬——「挺直的疲倦」，一手輕搭門框
- 服裝細節：父親的白色舊實驗服改成的大衣，微微偏大，袖口長過手腕，口袋微鼓
- 互動物件：手指搭在生鏽鐵門框上，指尖修長蒼白

**情緒張力**：★★★★☆ — 她像從另一個時間線走進來的人，白色在灰暗世界中刺眼得令人不安

**選擇理由**：女主角的第一印象決定整部作品的情感走向。逆光白色剪影是最強的視覺記憶點——觀者會記住「那個站在門口的白色影子」。

> **Prompt A（暖色生活動畫風）**：
> A young woman in an oversized white coat standing in a doorway, backlit by soft fluorescent corridor light creating a luminous silhouette. Shoulder-length black hair, steady dark eyes peering through the door gap. One pale hand resting on the rusted iron doorframe. The white coat glows warmly against the dim interior. Painterly soft edges, ethereal atmosphere like a ghost from a gentle dream, Violet Evergarden-style backlighting with warm diffusion.

> **Prompt B（霓虹賽博朋克風）**：
> Cyberpunk dramatic entrance — a woman in white lab coat standing in a dark metal doorframe, harsh fluorescent backlight creating a stark silhouette. Underground corridor behind her with exposed pipes and flickering lights. Her dark eyes cut through shadow, unnervingly steady. White coat almost glowing against grimy industrial surroundings. High contrast chiaroscuro, Ghost in the Shell mystery, cold blue-white rim light outlining her figure against darkness.

> **Prompt C（極簡向量插畫風）**：
> Flat vector illustration of a female silhouette in white standing within a dark rectangular doorframe. Simple geometric composition: black rectangle frame, bright white figure, grey corridor beyond with a single fluorescent bar. Hair as clean black shape, eyes as two dark determined points. Extreme contrast between white coat and dark surroundings. Three-color palette: pure white, charcoal black, cool grey. Bold editorial framing.

Pale cream skin, simplified black hair as smooth geometric curves. Barely visible lip curve suggested by a single thin line. White collar peeking from bottom edge. Minimal palette: cream, near-black, soft grey, one subtle rose accent at lips. Bold, confrontational editorial composition.

A small silver circle marks the memory chip location with radiating concentric rings suggesting activation. Simple black hair, pale face, white coat collar. Background split: dark grey left, lighter grey right creating breathing room. Four-color palette: charcoal, silver, cream, soft blue rings. Meditative editorial mood, clean geometric curves.
