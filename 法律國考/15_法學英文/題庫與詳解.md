# 法學英文 題庫與詳解

> 12題：術語配對、閱讀測驗、法律文件分析。附中文解說。零基礎適用。

## 第1題：基礎術語配對

### 題目
Match each legal term with its correct definition:

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| 1. Plaintiff | A. A formal written statement by a witness, sworn under oath |
| 2. Defendant | B. The party who initiates a lawsuit |
| 3. Affidavit | C. A court order requiring a person to appear and testify |
| 4. Subpoena | D. The party against whom a lawsuit is brought |
| 5. Jurisdiction | E. The authority of a court to hear and decide a case |

### 考點分析
基礎訴訟程序英文術語，國考必考範圍

### 參考答案
1-B, 2-D, 3-A, 4-C, 5-E

### 中文解說
- **Plaintiff（原告）**：提起訴訟之一方。刑事案件中則稱 prosecutor（檢察官）
- **Defendant（被告）**：被起訴之一方
- **Affidavit（宣誓書/切結書）**：經宣誓之書面證詞，具有法律效力
- **Subpoena（傳票）**：法院命令，要求證人出庭作證。源自拉丁文 sub poena（在處罰之下）
- **Jurisdiction（管轄權）**：法院審理案件之權限，包含事物管轄與地域管轄

### 延伸變化
- Appellant（上訴人）vs Appellee/Respondent（被上訴人）
- Petition（聲請書）vs Complaint（起訴狀）

### 常見錯誤
- 混淆 affidavit（宣誓書）與 deposition（庭外證詞筆錄）
- 混淆 subpoena 與 summons（傳喚通知）

## 第2題：憲法概念術語

### 題目
Choose the correct term for each description:

(1) The principle that the government must follow fair procedures before depriving a person of life, liberty, or property:
A. Equal Protection  B. Due Process  C. Habeas Corpus  D. Judicial Review

(2) A court order requiring the government to justify a person's detention:
A. Injunction  B. Mandamus  C. Habeas Corpus  D. Certiorari

(3) The power of courts to declare legislative acts unconstitutional:
A. Stare Decisis  B. Judicial Review  C. Separation of Powers  D. Supremacy Clause

### 考點分析
美國憲法基本概念，常出現在閱讀測驗中

### 參考答案
(1) B  (2) C  (3) B

### 中文解說
- **Due Process（正當法律程序）**：政府剝奪人民生命、自由、財產前須踐行正當程序。分為 procedural due process（程序正當）與 substantive due process（實質正當）
- **Habeas Corpus（人身保護令）**：拉丁文意為「呈交其身」，要求政府說明拘禁之合法理由，為最古老的人權保障制度
- **Judicial Review（司法審查/違憲審查）**：源自 Marbury v. Madison (1803)，法院有權宣告法律違憲

### 延伸變化
- Stare Decisis（遵循前例原則）
- Certiorari（調卷令——美國最高法院選案機制）

### 常見錯誤
- 混淆 habeas corpus（人身保護令）與 injunction（禁制令）
- 不知 certiorari 為美國最高法院的裁量上訴制度

## 第3題：契約法閱讀測驗

### 題目
Read the passage and answer the questions:

> "Under common law, a valid contract requires four elements: offer, acceptance, consideration, and the intention to create legal relations. Consideration refers to something of value exchanged between the parties — it need not be adequate, but it must be sufficient. A promise to make a gift, lacking consideration, is generally unenforceable. However, under the doctrine of promissory estoppel, a promise may be enforced if the promisor should reasonably expect the promisee to rely on it, and injustice can only be avoided by enforcement."

(1) According to the passage, which is NOT a required element of a valid contract?
A. Offer  B. Consideration  C. Capacity  D. Acceptance

(2) What does "consideration" mean in contract law?
A. Careful thought  B. Something of value exchanged  C. A written agreement  D. Legal capacity

(3) Under what doctrine can a promise without consideration be enforced?
A. Estoppel by deed  B. Promissory estoppel  C. Res judicata  D. Caveat emptor

### 考點分析
英美契約法基本要素，閱讀理解能力

### 參考答案
(1) C  (2) B  (3) B

### 中文解說
- **Offer（要約）、Acceptance（承諾）**：與我國民法概念相近
- **Consideration（對價/約因）**：英美法特有概念，指雙方交換有價值之事物。我國民法無此要件。"need not be adequate but must be sufficient"意指不要求等值但須有法律上之價值
- **Promissory Estoppel（允諾禁反言）**：當受允諾人合理信賴允諾並據此行動，即使無對價，法院可強制執行該允諾以避免不公正
- **Capacity（行為能力）**：雖也是契約有效要件，但文中未提及，故為正確答案

### 常見錯誤
- 將 consideration 按日常英文理解為「考慮」
- 不知道 promissory estoppel 為 consideration 的例外

## 第4題：刑法術語

### 題目
Fill in the blanks with the correct legal terms:

The mental state required to commit a crime is called __(1)__, while the physical act is called __(2)__. In criminal law, the prosecution must prove guilt __(3)__, which is a higher standard than the civil standard of __(4)__.

A. actus reus  B. beyond a reasonable doubt  C. mens rea  D. preponderance of the evidence

### 考點分析
刑法基本概念的英文表達

### 參考答案
(1) C — mens rea  (2) A — actus reus  (3) B — beyond a reasonable doubt  (4) D — preponderance of the evidence

### 中文解說
- **Mens rea（犯意/犯罪心態）**：拉丁文「有罪之心」，對應我國刑法之故意(intent)與過失(negligence)概念
- **Actus reus（犯罪行為）**：拉丁文「有罪之行為」，指構成犯罪之客觀行為要素
- **Beyond a reasonable doubt（超越合理懷疑）**：刑事案件之舉證標準，約對應95%以上確信度
- **Preponderance of the evidence（優勢證據）**：民事案件之舉證標準，僅需證明「比較可能為真」（>50%）

### 延伸變化
- Clear and convincing evidence（明確且令人信服之證據）——介於兩者之間
- Burden of proof（舉證責任）vs Standard of proof（證明標準）

### 常見錯誤
- 混淆 mens rea 與 motive（動機）
- 不知刑事與民事舉證標準差異的英文表達

## 第5題：侵權法閱讀測驗

### 題目
Read the passage and answer:

> "In tort law, negligence is established when four elements are proven: duty of care, breach of that duty, causation, and damages. The 'reasonable person' standard is used to determine whether the defendant breached the duty of care. Strict liability, by contrast, does not require proof of fault — the defendant is liable regardless of how careful they were. Product liability cases often apply strict liability, holding manufacturers responsible for defective products even without proof of negligence."

(1) How many elements must be proven for negligence?
A. 3  B. 4  C. 5  D. 6

(2) What standard is used to evaluate breach of duty?
A. Beyond reasonable doubt  B. Reasonable person  C. Clear and convincing  D. Strict scrutiny

(3) In strict liability, what does the plaintiff NOT need to prove?
A. Damages  B. Causation  C. Fault  D. Defect

### 參考答案
(1) B  (2) B  (3) C

### 中文解說
- **Negligence（過失侵權）**四要素：duty（注意義務）、breach（違反義務）、causation（因果關係）、damages（損害）
- **Reasonable person standard（理性人標準）**：以一般理性人之行為標準判斷被告是否違反注意義務，類似我國「善良管理人注意義務」
- **Strict liability（嚴格責任/無過失責任）**：不問行為人有無過失均須負責，常用於產品責任(product liability)、危險活動(abnormally dangerous activities)

### 常見錯誤
- 混淆 strict liability（無過失責任）與 absolute liability（絕對責任）
- 以為 strict liability 不需證明因果關係（仍然需要）

## 第6題：公司法英文

### 題目
Choose the best answer:

(1) A "corporation" is characterized by:
A. Unlimited liability of owners  B. Separate legal personality  C. Single ownership requirement  D. No need for registration

(2) "Piercing the corporate veil" means:
A. Creating a new company  B. Holding shareholders personally liable  C. Dissolving a corporation  D. Issuing new shares

(3) A "fiduciary duty" requires:
A. Payment of taxes  B. Acting in the best interest of another party  C. Filing annual reports  D. Maintaining minimum capital

### 參考答案
(1) B  (2) B  (3) B

### 中文解說
- **Corporation（公司/法人）**：具有獨立法律人格(separate legal personality)，股東僅負有限責任(limited liability)
- **Piercing the corporate veil（揭穿公司面紗）**：例外情形下否認公司獨立人格，使股東就公司債務負個人責任。對應我國公司法第154條第2項
- **Fiduciary duty（受信義務/忠實義務）**：董事對公司負有忠實義務(duty of loyalty)與注意義務(duty of care)

### 常見錯誤
- 不知 pierce the corporate veil 的意思
- 混淆 fiduciary duty 與 contractual duty

## 第7題：國際法英文閱讀

### 題目
Read and answer:

> "The principle of sovereign immunity holds that a foreign state cannot be sued in the courts of another state without its consent. However, the restrictive theory of sovereign immunity, adopted by most countries today, distinguishes between acts jure imperii (public/governmental acts) and acts jure gestionis (private/commercial acts). Under this theory, immunity is granted only for governmental acts."

(1) Under the restrictive theory, a foreign state is NOT immune when it engages in:
A. Military operations  B. Commercial activities  C. Diplomatic functions  D. Legislative acts

(2) "Jure imperii" refers to:
A. Commercial acts  B. Governmental acts  C. Private acts  D. Judicial acts

### 參考答案
(1) B  (2) B

### 中文解說
- **Sovereign immunity（主權豁免/國家豁免）**：一國不受他國法院管轄之原則
- **Restrictive theory（限制豁免理論）**：現代通說，僅公權力行為(jure imperii)享有豁免，商業行為(jure gestionis)不享有豁免。我國《外國國家豁免法》(2018)亦採此立場
- **Jure imperii**（拉丁文）：以統治權行事＝公權力行為
- **Jure gestionis**（拉丁文）：以管理權行事＝私經濟行為

### 常見錯誤
- 混淆 jure imperii 與 jure gestionis 的方向
- 以為主權豁免是絕對的

## 第8題：法律文件分析——契約條款

### 題目
Read the following contract clause and answer:

> "FORCE MAJEURE: Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations under this Agreement if such failure or delay results from circumstances beyond the reasonable control of that party, including but not limited to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, earthquake, flood, or governmental action. The affected party shall promptly notify the other party and use reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects."

(1) What does "Force Majeure" mean?
A. Breach of contract  B. Superior force / unforeseeable circumstances  C. Termination clause  D. Penalty provision

(2) Which of the following is NOT listed as a force majeure event?
A. Pandemic  B. Earthquake  C. Market downturn  D. War

(3) What must the affected party do?
A. Immediately terminate the contract  B. Pay damages  C. Notify the other party and mitigate effects  D. Seek arbitration

### 參考答案
(1) B  (2) C  (3) C

### 中文解說
- **Force Majeure（不可抗力）**：法文，意為「上級力量」。指非當事人所能控制之不可預見事件，致無法履行契約義務時得免責
- 條款列舉：acts of God（天災）、war（戰爭）、terrorism（恐怖主義）、pandemic（大流行病）、earthquake（地震）、flood（洪水）、governmental action（政府行為）
- **Market downturn（市場低迷）**通常不構成不可抗力
- 受影響方之義務：promptly notify（即時通知）+ mitigate（減輕損害）

### 常見錯誤
- 以為不可抗力免除所有責任（僅免除遲延或不履行之責任）
- 忽略通知義務與減損義務

## 第9題：人權法英文

### 題目
Read and answer:

> "The European Court of Human Rights applies the 'margin of appreciation' doctrine, which grants member states a degree of discretion in implementing Convention rights. The breadth of this margin depends on several factors, including the nature of the right, the existence of a European consensus, and the context of the case. Rights that are considered fundamental, such as the prohibition of torture, allow virtually no margin of appreciation."

(1) The "margin of appreciation" gives states:
A. Absolute freedom  B. Some discretion  C. No flexibility  D. Veto power

(2) The margin is narrower when:
A. The right is less important  B. There is no consensus  C. The right is fundamental  D. The state is larger

### 參考答案
(1) B  (2) C

### 中文解說
- **Margin of appreciation（裁量餘地原則）**：歐洲人權法院給予會員國在實踐公約權利時之一定裁量空間。類似我國釋憲實務中的「立法裁量」概念
- 影響裁量幅度之因素：(1)權利之性質——越核心越窄；(2)歐洲共識——有共識則裁量越窄；(3)案件脈絡
- 禁止酷刑(prohibition of torture)幾乎無裁量空間

### 常見錯誤
- 以為 margin of appreciation 允許國家完全不遵守公約
- 不知此為歐洲人權法院特有概念

## 第10題：仲裁法英文

### 題目
Read the arbitration clause and answer:

> "Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this contract shall be finally settled by arbitration administered by the International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) in accordance with its Arbitration Rules. The arbitral tribunal shall consist of three arbitrators. The seat of arbitration shall be Singapore. The language of the arbitration shall be English. The award shall be final and binding upon both parties."

(1) How many arbitrators will hear the dispute?
A. 1  B. 2  C. 3  D. 5

(2) "The award shall be final and binding" means:
A. The award can be appealed  B. The parties must comply and cannot appeal on merits  C. The award is advisory  D. The award expires after one year

(3) What is the "seat" of arbitration?
A. Physical meeting location  B. Legal place determining procedural law  C. Language of proceedings  D. Governing law of contract

### 參考答案
(1) C  (2) B  (3) B

### 中文解說
- **Arbitration（仲裁）**：替代訴訟之爭端解決方式，裁決(award)具有終局拘束力
- **ICC（國際商會）**：世界最主要之國際商務仲裁機構之一
- **Seat of arbitration（仲裁地）**：非指實際開會地點，而是決定仲裁程序法之法律所在地。本題仲裁地為新加坡，適用新加坡仲裁法
- **Final and binding（終局且有拘束力）**：當事人不得就實體問題上訴，僅能在極有限情形下（如程序瑕疵）聲請撤銷

### 常見錯誤
- 混淆 seat（仲裁地）與 venue（開會地點）
- 以為仲裁裁決可以上訴

## 第11題：智慧財產法英文

### 題目
Choose the correct answer:

(1) "Fair use" in copyright law considers all of the following EXCEPT:
A. Purpose of use  B. Nature of the copyrighted work  C. Nationality of the author  D. Effect on the market

(2) A "patent troll" refers to:
A. An inventor who improves existing patents  B. An entity that acquires patents solely to sue for infringement  C. A government patent examiner  D. A company that licenses patents fairly

(3) "Trade dress" protects:
A. Employee uniforms  B. The overall visual appearance of a product  C. Trade secrets  D. Domain names

### 參考答案
(1) C  (2) B  (3) B

### 中文解說
- **Fair use（合理使用）**四因素：(1)使用目的與性質(purpose and character)；(2)著作之性質(nature of work)；(3)使用之質量與比例(amount used)；(4)對市場之影響(market effect)。作者國籍非考量因素
- **Patent troll（專利蟑螂/專利流氓）**：不從事實際生產，僅收購專利後向他人提起侵權訴訟以獲取和解金或賠償金之實體
- **Trade dress（商業外觀）**：產品之整體視覺印象，包括包裝設計、顏色搭配、店面裝潢等，受商標法保護

### 常見錯誤
- 以為 fair use 是無限制的免費使用
- 不知 trade dress 的概念

## 第12題：綜合法律英文閱讀

### 題目
Read the passage and answer:

> "The rule of law requires that all persons, institutions, and entities are accountable to laws that are publicly promulgated, equally enforced, and independently adjudicated. It demands that the laws be clear, publicized, stable, and applied evenly. The process of enacting, administering, and enforcing the law must be accessible, fair, and efficient. The rule of law is the foundation upon which democratic societies and market economies are built."

(1) According to the passage, laws must be:
A. Secret and flexible  B. Clear, publicized, and stable  C. Applied only to citizens  D. Changed frequently

(2) "Independently adjudicated" means:
A. Judged by the executive branch  B. Decided by independent courts  C. Voted on by the public  D. Reviewed by legislators

(3) The passage states the rule of law is the foundation of:
A. Authoritarian regimes  B. Military power  C. Democratic societies and market economies  D. Religious institutions

### 參考答案
(1) B  (2) B  (3) C

### 中文解說
- **Rule of law（法治）**：所有人與機構均受法律約束。法治的核心要素：(1)法律須公開(publicly promulgated)；(2)平等適用(equally enforced)；(3)由獨立機關裁判(independently adjudicated)
- **Accountable（負有責任的/應負責的）**：所有人、機構、實體均須對法律負責
- **Promulgated（公布的）**：法律須經正式公布始生效力
- 法治為民主社會(democratic societies)與市場經濟(market economies)之基礎

### 延伸變化
- Rule of law vs Rule by law（法治 vs 以法而治）的區別
- Separation of powers（權力分立）的英文論述

### 常見錯誤
- 混淆 rule of law（法治）與 rule by law（依法統治——威權體制亦可）
- 不知 adjudicate 意為「裁判/審判」
